thyme by joaomoreno

Thyme is a timer for OS X. It's as simple as that.

Keep track of the time you spend in your tasks with Thyme. It gives you everything you would need in a barebones time tracking application, while being unobtrusive, placing itself on the OS X status bar.

Install

Just drag to the Applications folder.
